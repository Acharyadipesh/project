About Us

Welcome to Hotel Annapurna

Hotel Annapurna is a luxury hotel located in the heart of Kathmandu, Nepal. With breathtaking views of the surrounding mountains and a commitment to exceptional service, we offer an unforgettable experience for our guests.

Our hotel features beautifully appointed rooms and suites, exquisite dining options, state-of-the-art conference facilities, and a range of leisure amenities including a spa, fitness center, and swimming pool.

Whether you're visiting for business or leisure, Hotel Annapurna is the perfect choice for your stay in Kathmandu. We look forward to welcoming you and providing you with a truly memorable experience.
